⟢**Deity Sheet**⟣
⊱Name: Salem 
⊱Symbols: Buck Skulls With flowers weaved through the antlers
⊱God of: Conspiracies and Monsters
⊱Deity Connections: 
[[Main Plot/Deities/Calor\|Calor]]: Close friend, often gives gifts to Calor, usually consisting of small creatures
[[Main Plot/Deities/Tabeth\|Tabeth]]: Parent figure
⊱Character Connections: [[Cadmus\|Cadmus]]: Directly, Visits often and watches over
⊱Description:
-height:10’8”
he is slender with deep grayish-black fur, along with some brown fur, he has a buck skull for the top of his head, most assume its a mask but no, his eyes are jet black with white pupils, he is canine and Cervine in appearance, possessing both Hooves and Clawed paws, they have what appear to be bones on his sides and upper arms. 
Personality:
  Secretive and sensitive, Salem is often quite secluded when thinking up plans, he's not malicious but most assume him to be, he's very sensitive when it comes to his creations, friends and family, and will kill to protect them
⊱ Holidays; The Feast of whispers (A yearly holiday in which people join together to conspire about their plans for the upcoming year, whether bad or good, this is a very important day and most are real secretive, it's custom to whisper during this day)
(It is significant for his followers due to the fact this is the day Salem tends to choose a new high priest to replace an older one, often making an appearance during the feast)
⊱Backstory:
 Salem was born a deity, Created by Humans starting their own secret plans, He rules over the realm of conspiracy by influencing or helping humans come up with the plans, He's been around for just under 100’000 years, when humans gained a complex enough language to start conspiring, yet still he's one of if not THE youngest god, as he grew, he made Monsters, creatures that he assumed humans would love, only to see humans hate or dislike them, he assisted in the creation of many as well, but his favorites are Dragons, Piglins and creepers, often taking care of those the most, despite his age, he's still very sensitive and seeks comfort in the other gods.
