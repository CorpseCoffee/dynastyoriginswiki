★ Skills: Making totems and carving wood. Great with knives as tools and blades. The knowledge and writing of runes and sigils. Proficient in written spells. Sturdy and strong build.
★ Weaknesses: His skin is very sensitive in most places due to scaring. His stamina is poor due to age.
★ Personality: A calm and often tired man. He works well with his hands, valuing skill and effort. He has a very clear understanding of the risks of magic, he takes a cautious and practiced approach. 
★ Likes: The smell of wood, birds, stew
★ Dislikes: Warm weather, sand, blood
★ Backstory: He was adopted by an elvin family and raised in Purina. He became scared and bitter of death, he would never see his family truly grow with him. He drowned himself in books about lasting youth. However, in a kingdom that didn't have the resources for such magic, he had to turn to runes. He carved symbol upon symbol into his skin. Lines of magic to ensure a long life. After this disparate move he started to learn more about the craft itself. The runes were imperfect, he had to carve several more over his lifetime to sustain himself. He learned more as he went and eventually moved to Radicis to explore it further. His practice paved in blood, but nonetheless he honed it. He's since focused more on supporting Radicis as an elder. Using his knowledge to carve into wood and stone instead.
