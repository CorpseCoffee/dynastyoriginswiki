**★ Skills:** potion making, crafting, storytelling
**★ Other Abilities:** Strength, ability to take fall damage
**★ Weaknesses:** Angry or frustrated emotions (causes his magic to be messed up), anything that hard or solid that covers his head (helmets and such, hoods are okay since they are more flexible and thin), any blows to his head.
**★ Personality:** Introverted and easily agitated when first met, but pretty outgoing to people he knows.
**★ Likes:** Creating things, taking walks, finding random things, alchemy, meditating, reading
**★ Dislikes:** Anything that disrupts whatever he’s doing
**★ Other Details:** He isn’t the smartest person, but still is very talented in the skills he has. He also comes off a little threatening since he is more quieter at first.

**★ Backstory:** He born to a village that heavily despised any sorts of magic and such. One day, while Evander and his family were out, he threw a tantrum and revealed to the village that he had powers. He was threatened to be arrested, burned, or exorcised by most of the village, so his family decided to abandoned him and left him out in the woods to survive on his own. That was until he was found by Magnus, who took him to Radicus and helped him til he was old enough to live on his own. Since then, he has found joy in being alone. So he spends most of his time trying not to draw much attention to himself.
