★ Backstory:

When Owlet gained sentience about 5 Million years ago, they were.. very lonely. They didn't know whether there was anyone else like them out there, and for the longest time there genuinely wasn't. They'd take over different animal carcasses, hunting for survival and thinking.. Pondering.. Wondering..

It didn't take them long to find out their biggest fear was being the only sentient being in existence. They found this out by accidentally looking at their own reflection and falling victim to their powers (if you look in their eyes, you hallucinate your worst fears).

When people came around, Owlet was deep in forests and caves. He did not know of their sentience and whenever one happened to walk by, owlet thought of that person as nothing more than a simple creature, and a potential food source. Owlet has killed many people not knowing they were sentient.

One day they were in a cave, when suddenly, someone appeared. At the time Owlet was using the carcass of a bat and fell off the cave ceiling in shock, when suddenly this strange creature manifested in front of them. They didn't know that said creature was Salem, the deity of Conspiracies, but Owlet knew that this creature was sentient because he tried to speak to them. Even though Salem didn't stay in that cave very long at all and Owlet never understood the attempt at communication, now that they knew there was at least one other sentient life form out there, they had hope of there possibly being more. 

About 60 years before current time, however, is when her life took a drastic turn. Owlet spotted another Person in the woods, riding a skeleton horse. At the time Owlet did not know that this person was Rusty, the current sheriff of Solaris. To Owlet they were dinner.

When Owlet attacked Rusty, however, a mysterious figure ([[Quastratus\|Quastratus]]) came to them. The two started communicating, and though Owlet did not understand the words, he understood that this was a language. And that Owlet had just killed a sentient being.

They froze, trying to comprehend their situation, as they thought back to all the times they killed a person, doubting everything. In all this panic Owlet didn't even realize that Quastratus, in grief of Rusty having been killed, charged up a powerful strike.

It hit owlet right in the face, leaving a deep scar across their face, neck, and chest. The strike had taken out their left eye.

After several weeks they started looking for more signs of sentience. Quastratus eventually came to apologize, since he realized that Owlet was a sentient creature who made a mistake rather than a heartless monster.

Owlet eventually stumbled upon Faearum, pretending to be a child in order to not scare the locals. They were taken in by a kind family (if i win leader, this will be the royal family). And they have been living in Faearum for about 60 years now.
