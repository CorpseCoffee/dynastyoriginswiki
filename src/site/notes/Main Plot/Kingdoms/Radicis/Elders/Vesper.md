★ Backstory: Vesper was born in a small cabin beyond any kingdom’s reach, long before Radicis existed. At seventeen, he became a wandering trader, traveling through every kingdom and learning their laws. He discovered magic was universally outlawed. Driven by curiosity, he traded emeralds for a mysterious amulet from an unknown entity. During his travels, he befriended Cassius, who later betrayed him after discovering the amulet. Cassius murdered Vesper’s family and killed him during their final duel. The amulet resurrected Vesper as an undead being. Centuries later, Vesper met the unnamed founder of Radicis, the strongest mage ever known. Inspired by his warmth and vision, Vesper aided the kingdom’s early growth and later became one of its eternal elders
