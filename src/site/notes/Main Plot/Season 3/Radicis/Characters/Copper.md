★ Backstory: Copper grew up in a very small village with other cowfolk. He had a lot of responsibilities to help maintain and feed the village. As much as he thrived on helping others, he felt stuck. The same few buildings and land for ages, nothing but people shouting at him for tasks. Dispute being born here just like everyone else, he never felt like he belonged. He wanted to find something bigger to work towards and people that actually felt like home. Once a few of the village children came of age to help out, he figured it was a good time leave. He packed everything he owned in a travel pack and began saying his goodbyes. Many meet him with scolding or worry. He eased them before setting out. Eventually arriving in Radicis, where he was greeted by a curious dragonfolk.
