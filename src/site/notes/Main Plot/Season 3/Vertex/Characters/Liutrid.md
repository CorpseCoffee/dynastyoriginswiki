Liutrid
➤ Name: Liutrid
➤ Alias: Liut

➤ Current Kingdom: [[Main Plot/Season 3/Vertex/Vertex\|Vertex]]
➤ Kingdom Born In:  Vertex

➤ Origin: Llama

➤ Age: 17
➤ Birthday: 16/09

➤ Pronouns: She/her
➤ Sexuality: Lesbian, Aroace
➤ Open for ships: No



➤ Occupation: Agriculture, Livestock (Farming)
➤ Connections: [[Main Plot/Season 3/Vertex/Characters/Kiwi\|Kiwi]] (Friendly rivalry)

➤ Skills: Farming, building
➤ Other Abilities: Oddly good at chess.
➤ Weaknesses: Emotionally weak, lacks awareness.

➤ Personality: Liut- a stubborn farmer that resides in Vertex. Raised with the belief that labour grows rewards, she’s very determined and stubbornly loyal to her passion, which brings a sense of fatigue that never quite fades away. She’s burning out, but refuses to admit it,pushing towards complete exhaustion. 

➤ Likes: Sheep, carpets, isolation, apples.
➤ Dislikes: Big masses of people, forced interaction.
➤ Other Details: Despite her seemingly never ending hunger for physical labour, she also enjoys writing and reading literature.

➤ Backstory: Liut was born and raised in the Vertex territory.  Her mother passed when Liut was young- so she was raised as an only child in an already impoverished family. Her family was a long line of mountain swept living llamas- who joined the Vertex kingdom during their time. 
Albeit her family has lived in Vertex for generations, their roots come from unknown traces beyond Vertex’s current territory. Liut was especially interested in her family tree and literature, but was kept away from reading and education in favour of labouring in the family farm- to which the idealogy stuck.


➤ Appearance: Softly tanned skin, her face is complimented by dirty blonde hair. Her appearance is that of what appears to be an average human- having her tail and ears tucked in. 
- Height: 5’3
- Notable features: Little ears poke out from her hair occasionally.
- Clothing: A plaid jacket, with a shirt underneath and worker jeans, stained with dirt after longtime use. She wears brown boots.
