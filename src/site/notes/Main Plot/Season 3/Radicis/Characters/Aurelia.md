# Aurelia
★ Name: Aurelia  
★ Alias: Reliquary, The Hollow Chest (rumor only)
★ Current Kingdom: [[Main Plot/Season 3/Radicis/Radicis\|Radicis]]
★ Kingdom Born In: ???
★ Origin: Chestling
★ Age: Unknown (physically appears ~20)
★ Birthday: October 3rd
★ Pronouns: she/her
★ Sexuality: Unlabeled
★ Open for ships: Yes
★ Occupation: Apprentice 

**★ Connections:**
[Arch Mage Magnus](Magnus) — Aurelia was discovered wandering near the Radicis outskirts carrying unstable enchanted debris inside her body. Magnus permitted her to remain in Radicis. She views him as her protector, though she does not fully understand why.

**★ Skills:**
- Exceptional organization and memory for physical objects
- Precise and careful handling of fragile or enchanted items
- Quiet movement and observational awareness
- Strong spatial intuition, especially regarding containment and placement
- Patient and methodical worker

**★ Other Abilities:**

*Dimensional Reliquary (Chest Core)*
Aurelia possesses a visible chasm in the center of her chest. Inside exists a small, unstable dimensional storage space. She can reach into it and retrieve objects she has stored there regardless of physical depth.
- Items remain preserved in their stored state
- She instinctively knows if something inside her shifts or destabilizes
- Enchanted objects feel “heavier” to hold internally, even without physical weight
- She sometimes stores things unconsciously when distressed

*Residual Awareness*
She occasionally experiences faint impressions or emotional echoes from strongly enchanted objects she carries, though she cannot control or interpret them fully.

**★ Weaknesses:**

*Physical Fragility*
Her body is less durable than most. Physical damage, especially to her chest area, causes extreme distress and instability.

*Heat Sensitivity*
Hot temperatures hurt more, and make her feel panicky.

*Identity Instability*
She sometimes questions whether her thoughts and emotions are truly her own, or remnants of the objects she carries.

*Limited Combat Ability*
She has no inherent offensive capabilities and relies entirely on avoidance or assistance.

**★ Personality:**
Aurelia is gentle, quiet, and observant, with a natural tendency toward stillness. She speaks softly and deliberately, often pausing before answering questions as though sorting through invisible shelves in her mind. She is deeply curious about people but hesitant to intrude. She forms attachments slowly but permanently, and once she considers someone “hers,” she becomes quietly protective.
She has an unusual relationship with ownership—she does not see objects as possessions, but as things entrusted to her care. She does not hoard out of greed, but out of instinct and comfort.
Despite her calm nature, she carries a subtle unease, as if she is always listening to something no one else can hear.

**★ Likes:**
- Being entrusted with objects
- Quiet libraries and workshops
- Gentle voices
- Small, intricate items
- Being useful
- Watching others work

**★ Dislikes:**
- Sudden loud noises
- Being touched near her chest unexpectedly
- Extreme cold
- Being treated like an object rather than a person
- Losing track of something entrusted to her

**★ Other Details:**
- She does not need to eat, but mimics the behavior occasionally
- Her internal storage space is larger than physically possible, but not infinite
- She sometimes forgets when or why she stored certain objects
- When distressed, the interior of her chest emits faint, flickering green light

**★ Backstory:**
No one witnessed Aurelia’s creation.

She was first seen wandering alone along the outer wetlands of Radicis, barefoot and silent, with fragments of broken enchanted tools appearing and disappearing inside the hollow space in her chest. The magic surrounding her was unstable, flickering unpredictably, as though unsure whether it still belonged in the world.
When approached, she did not resist. She did not speak. She simply watched.
Arch Mage Magnus allowed her to remain in Radicis.  She never asked why she existed.
She simply held what others could not. Sometimes, she wonders if something was removed from her long ago—or if she was created to hold something that has not yet been found.

**★ Appearance:**
- Height: 5’4

*Notable features:*
  A vertical, hollow chasm runs through the center of her chest, its interior darker than shadow. Faint, shifting light occasionally flickers deep within, especially when she is emotional or actively retrieving something. Her movements are careful and precise, as if she is always aware of fragile contents within herself.
Her eyes are soft and reflective, often appearing slightly unfocused when she is thinking.
