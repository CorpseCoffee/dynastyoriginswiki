★ Backstory: A long time ago, a Deity going by the name of Genoth - The Deity of Hate and War was materialised into this world, born from the unresolved hate of sentient beings.
Quickly did his group of followers grow, and more madness grew betwixt them... 
They started to slaughter and cannibalise one another, a bloody cycle which angered Genoth as he was losing followers from this barbaric form of worship
And so Orion was created; one of Genoths angel servants, he was tasked to oversee the followers of Genoth as for them to not stir any more chaos within each other, and so he followed orders.
One day Genoth just... vanished... no one knew where he went even his highest ranking followers, and so the chaos returned and it engulfed even Orion, he vowed to never serve Genoth.. Centuries later Genoth awoke from his slumber, he was furious that one of his previously most loyal members had just forsaken him. 
He stripped him of his Deity-given powers and sent him down to the mortal realm as punishment, with scorched, damaged wings and no one by his side he swore to never be a subject of Genoths cruelty again... 
His time in the mortal world found him crashing upon a cold yet lush mountain in the midst of a storm, it went by the name of Vertex, and there he came across a tavern - the perfect place to hide from the rain and drown his fury and sorrows in a bottle, and so he did.
Not long after, a blonde man with a scar on his neck entered the tavern, he saw Orion as a new comer to Vertex and greeted him, his name was [[Michael\|Michael]].
They drank until the sun rose and they went their seperate ways, Orion enjoyed his company and figured that all of Vertex must be this welcoming, so he set up a temporary home on the Southern side of the mountain, and thats where we are in the present..
