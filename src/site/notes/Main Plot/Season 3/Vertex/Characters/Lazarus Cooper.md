★ Personality:

Most of his humor comes from self-deprecating jokes, ‘cause it’s not considered funny if you insult anyone else. Even with this, hypocritically, he’s still going to be hurt by ruder comments. (Don’t I do everything for you? Why won’t you like me? Maybe I am simply abhorrent.)
He… tries to be kinder, at least when in the presence of Eclipse, his group, and Dallas (or everyone that has any value to him).

★ Backstory:
Lazarus was an only child, living with his pa and ma. They were more or less happy- what you'd expect from an average family in Vertex.
Whose fault was it? The gods? But he’d worshipped them his entire life, they wouldn’t have done this. His fault? Perhaps he should’ve known better than to track dirt, and likely plague, back home to his frailer family. Maybe it was a freak accident; they were playing a card game, and life gave them a bad hand.
Faster than he could comprehend, when he was around 16, his parents passed from an unlabeled illness. The kid tried to give them a proper burial, but that just made him sick too. 
As he lay there, wishing he’d join them, he started to detest his name. No deity had tried to help him (and he did not think of his parents, for he was selfish). It was hardly fitting for him.
Cooper recovered. Life went on.
When he was around 19, a wolfwalker had joined Vertex with other followers of Salem. Desperately trying to find purpose, he joined them.
Eclipse and the others were kind enough to Cooper, he supposed. At least they trusted him to oversee the graveyard and funerals. He didn’t really feel better, but at least he could finally let his parents rest with dignity, knowing (hoping, praying) that Salem could watch over them.
