## Faearum
**The Foundation:**
The kingdom originally started as just a collection of different families of diverse people who were settling in the dark forest, both those who were native to it and those who came from afar, curious about its magic.

The families eventually started living closer together, and over the process of a few decades, Faearum eventually grew large and connected enough to need a political system, which a well-respected family of inhabitants took upon themselves to create. This family would become the royal family of Faearum.

The people of Faearum are interconnected with the natural magic, which is deeply intertwined within the forest and its inhabitants. Said magic affects the flora and fauna, enchanting everything within the biome. Faearum's vegetation is lush, whimsical and magical, and even its inhabitants usually carry the magic within them, it is rare to find ordinary people native to this kingdom.
[[Main Plot/Kingdoms/Faearum/Magical Trees\|Magical Trees]]

About a decade ago, the last remaining biological member of the royal family passed away. They were unable to have children of their own with their partner due to them being different species, so instead they used adoption to secure the heritage of their kingdom. To this day Faearum is prosperous and rich with magic.
