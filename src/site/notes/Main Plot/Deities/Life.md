⟢**God Sheet**⟣

⊱Name: Life

⊱Symbols: A cats paw touching a clover.

⊱God of: Life and Change

⊱Description: A white glowing cat in most forms, however Life is everywhere with no direct physical appearance.

⊱Personality: Benevolent and kind, a god willing to give anything for their world to thrive. They have sacrificed many things, however they always forgive no matter what has been done to them.

⊱Holidays: August 20th, day of change. Anything usually occurs on this day, but most people like to do this they normally don't. Such as exploring, trying new foods, or even painting pictures. The day of change is for you to grow out of your comfort zone, meet new people and do new things to change and shape who you are.

⊱Backstory: Life seemed to come out of nowhere. They fumbled between forms and voices for a while, wanting to interact with the world they were thrown into. Yet all they were met with was pain. No matter what they did or said, all of the people who cared left them alone.
They resented such things as Change. Change is what made people hate them and resent them, but over time they began to accept it as their own. They now celebrate the cycle of life and the versions of change, as change inevitably comes with life.
