⟢**God Sheet**⟣
⊱Name: Beathan

⊱Symbols: Hollow circle with rays coming from 6 points with a bundle of wheat crossing over a scythe

⊱God of: The Rising of the New Day and Growth of the Harvest

⊱Description: Said to be a benevolent god who brings the sun up and helps with growth of crops, within the past few centuries he has been forgotten, only a handful of proof of his existence exist as most was left as humanity farmed less and needed his help less.

⊱Personality: Kind, sassy and typically really chill and laid back until he has to actually do godly stuff then he's a stubborn bastard.

⊱Holidays: Solstice Harvest

**Solstice Harvest**
Once every year, on the brightest and longest day, all crops are harvested and offered as gifts to the god. Not many people do this as the god they are said to be offering to is forgotten, but some do leave out offerings of their yield each time they harvest instead.

⊱Backstory: One of the first gods created, helping early settlements grow there farms and bless farmers. As time went on and the settlements grew, they no longer needed the gods help, so they forgot him, leaving only a handful to remember him.

⊱Intentions and Motivation: After having been forgotten and almost completely erased from existence, Beathan decided to use some of his last remaining energy to give a robot consciousness, hoping it would revive his power so he could rise again to being a god worth worshipping.

⊱Extra Notes: The reason he's forgotten is because he's lazy and unreliable, sometimes forgetting to raise the sun on time (Hence why we have winter) and only blessings crops from time to time. This lead to most people loosing faith in him and slowly forgetting him.
