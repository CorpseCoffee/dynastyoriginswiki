★ Backstory: Rex was born in Zonas, the 8th circle to be exact, he was fairly poor, his father was overworked in the Mines and his family had to take care of the animals, One, His mother would cook, his older brother would handle the cows and he and his little sister would wander around.

Bandit Zao was Born on october 13th
5 years later his little sister was born and would give him the nickname “Bandito!”

One day a 6 Rex decided to take a stroll exploring, a child saw him playing and wanted to join, Rex refused he wanted to be alone and pointed out the kid had pushed his sister over previously, the child begged for a second chance and claimed he’d die for one, rex ignored him as he then walked over a ravine rex got over the log easy with his balance however the child continued begging for a second chance, Rex turned to yell but the child was gone, the child had fallen and died, Without a second chance, Rex was unsure how to react, he wasn’t the smartest individual so he walked home with the last words of the child “Everyone should have a second chance, even me!” stuck in his head.


2 years later rex was messing with the pigs, one pig then promptly Bit his ear and chipped it gaining a new fear for pigs

8 Years later on November 17th
a 16 year old Rex and his sister were out poaching and managed to kill a small fox, they then heard a bigger animal in the grass bandit turned to see his sister gone, behind him a warden stood, due to Rex being a feline his footsteps were quiet and he couldn’t be tracked, he could only stare in fear as his sister was ripped in half, Rex held the shotgun shaking as the warden walked off he ran home to see his farm destroyed, his brothers hat on the floor and his mothers dead body in the house, he takes his brothers hat and his mothers gun and runs to the hills, he believes since the warden could only have come from the caves his father is most likely dead, he stared down on zonas, in flames and destroyed.

4 months later
Rex now goes by Bandit Bandaroosh an outlaw known in the deserts, after a glass of milk he takes a stroll in the town until hes swiftly struck by the clouds, a woman has landed, she apologises and introduces herself as Angel Zeen.

6 months go by the two grow a close bond and Both fall for eachother but never say, one eventful day however Angel confesses, Bandit unsure how to react stares blankly, angel runs off believing bandit hates her now, bandit sits on a bench and considers a response, after 15 minutes he gets up to go to the tavern and confess back, hes greeted by angels dead corpse and 3 masked men laughing holding guns, Bandit kills all 3 men and swiftly leaves the town.

After walking for a couple days he finds a small town but something about it sparks something in him, hes here after killing 3 men, this here is his second chance.. Second chance, this place is for second chances hes sure of it, he befriends an individual called Mole man and Bandit purchases a small house near the edge of town, Bandit would then see a dog in-front of his house, his first pet, bandit would love it in solaris, and one day he would see a fox strolling infront, he wants a new pet, he jumps on top of the fox and it barks at him introduces themself as rusty, the two sparked a cousin like rivalry.

months after moving into solaris a 17 year old Bandit would walk into a man who would change his life forever, Cody Harper, Cody hated bandit at first for around 10 minutes until they started talking, they quickly became best friends and would do all sorts of adventures together like finding the blacksmith “Lebron James” Hammer, Helping Mr Tyler “The creator” Get all his seeds back and more, Eventually a 19 year old bandit entered the election for next Sheriff and Won, He appointed the person he respected most (And who he wanted to win) Rusty as his Deputy
