★ Backstory: Flint is a humanoid shaped copper automaton, created by a mage who was set on attempting to prove that magic could create life and sentience from non natural material. despite the insistence that the result the mage would be receiving would be much more different from what he imagined and may have severe unforeseen consequences, yet the warning was never acknowledged as the mage went through attempt after attempt to animate copper to sentience, although in private out of fear of interference by their peers. Soon finding the closer to humanoid the creatures appeared, the closer they gained to their desired result, until flint was finally configured and animated, an amalgamation of runes lining the inside and outside of his body as well as a magic that didn’t seem at all natural circulating throughout its body, echoing the warnings of the mages peers. in an attempt to cover and protect their work the mage proceeded to makeshift a “skin” of sorts out of thinner copper so if the creature was to be discovered it may be more accepted as humanoid, and thus avoid more suspicion of their activities. that skin proved to fade and peel with time as the mage continued to teach the seemingly alive mechanical array sentience, the construct choosing the out of place attire of a dress shirt and pants to cover the exposed etchings on its skin, the construct was taught speech as well as writing and arithmetic seemingly deemed essential to the idea of sentience, and giving it simple sorting tasks to keep it occupied and observe its motor functions, but as all things do Flint grew more and more curious of the space outside it’s carefully crafted “world” enough to one day attempt to wander outside of its home and loose its way wandering around the wilds outside for an age before seeing structures and wandering towards them before being caught in a heavy storm and finding itself frozen in place from then on.
