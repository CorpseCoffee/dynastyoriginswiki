## Radicis
**The Foundation:**
700 years ago because a mage was fed up with kingdoms kicking him out because of his magic practice. Radicis became a place for magic to exist without being ridiculed. At the start it was just him, but the kingdom grew. Reaching 50 residents of mages and witches, they decided to make something to enchant the land itself, a device that could enchant the very air you breath. After many sleepless nights they finally made it, and quickly the device became their most prizes possession. Time went on, things stayed the same. Leadership roles got passed around as their predecessors passed. Everything was peaceful.

**Marinis's Raid:**
[[Main Plot/Kingdoms/Marinis/Marinis\|Marinis]] attacked while the strongest mages and witches left for a political meeting. They burned, pillaged, and killed. The ultimately stumbled upon the device and stole it. Ever since Radicis hasn't seen or heard about the device, and to make things worse the Arch Mage had vanished.
