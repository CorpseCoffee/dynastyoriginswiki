⟢**Deity Sheet**⟣
⊱Name: Virilian, First Queen of [[Main Plot/Season 2/Viridis/Viridis\|Viridis]]
⊱Symbols: 
 - Quetzal Birds & a setting sun (Viridis banner)
⊱God of: Festivals & Birds

⊱Deity Connections: 
 - [[Main Plot/Deities/Beathan\|Beathan]] - Ex-partner 
⊱Character Connections: 
 - Queen Blue Spring Bangles
 - King Gray Autumn Bangles
 - all of Viridis’ royal family 

⊱Description:
 - a tall feather women with green feathers covering her body. Has another form that is a giant quetzal bird
⊱Personality:
  - Quiet and very sweet. Doesn’t always know how to hold her tongue which causes issues. Tries her best to be civil even if she hates it
⊱Holidays:
  - Viridis Festival - Normally held for the crowning of a new ruler or of a new year
⊱Backstory:
  - was born a deity along side Beathan and they decided to get married. Together they started Viridis but Beathan drew power hungry and Virilian sadly had to seal them away and erase the memory of him.
