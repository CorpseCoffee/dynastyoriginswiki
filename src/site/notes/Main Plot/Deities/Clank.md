⊱Description?
  Reddish hair braided into a man bun. Long braided beard, tan skin, pointed ears, red eyes, muscular build. On the shorter side.
⊱Personality:
  They're always up for a few drinks. Loves to forge. Their goal is to craft the best sword in history (He already has). Always trying to improve his craft. Likes to crack a joke from time to time. Usually in a cheerful mood.
⊱Holidays:
  The day of the forge. Long ago he locked himself in his forge for a month long crafting day and night. When he finally emerged he had crafted the greatest sword in existence. Blacksmiths all over the world lock themselves in their forge for the whole day the day he finished as a tribute and to try to craft their own masterpieces.
⊱Backstory:
He was born when the first piece of metal materialized in the world. He used that metal to make all sorts of things. One day he got bored of doing this craft all by himself so he went down and taught the mortals how to do it. He named it blacksmithing and from that day he was named the god of the forge and metals

More about him: Ever since the day he was born into the world he had one goal. Make the greatest armor and weapons ever created. Some are used by the gods others hero’s long forgotten. When he is not working they are drinking. Though they seem to never be able to get drunk. As of late they have fallen into a sort of a slump. While they don’t show it on the outside, but on the inside he is struggling wondering if his skills are good enough anymore.
