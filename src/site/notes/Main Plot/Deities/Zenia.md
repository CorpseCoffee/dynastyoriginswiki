⊱Name: Zenia
⊱Symbols: A Partridge carrying a bundle of berries and hay in its beak 
⊱God of: Agriculture and Fertility
⊱Deity Connections: 
-Life: Friends, also working together in times where Zenia needs it to ensure a sick or weak life brought into the world would make it (although the latter being really rare, instead she more so just enjoys hanging around [[Main Plot/Deities/Life\|Life]], getting along well)

⊱Character Connections: 
 -True Mother to [[Feather\|Feather]], Having placed him with the family he grew up with, she loves and cares  for him deeply as she now guides him through the afterlife

⊱Description:
-Her main form is a human with tan skin, Long brown and green hair,And yellow eyes (clothing shown in picture)
She does posess a second form, an Anthropomorphic rabbit with gray fur and yellow eyes, (will be shown when i make her art)
⊱Personality:
  She’s caring,Loving and refuses to back down,she has a deep love for those she considers close,Taking care of her son even after death,even though she tried helping him during death
⊱Holidays: 
 Festival of new growth:This holiday takes place around the first week of spring,lasting a full week,The festival celebrates new growth,Both in families and crops, Even personal growth or growth in couples
⊱Backstory: She was born a deity, growing up around the other deities, She takes care of the agriculture of the world as well as blessing those who may need it with the gift of fertility, Making growing families and ensuring they are well off within their communities, at least regarding food, working closely with Life to help ensure a new life reaches the world safely.
