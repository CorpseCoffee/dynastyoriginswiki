★ Backstory:

Mirage was born many, many years ago (nobody is sure when) in a far away forest that resembled an old growth. He was abandoned by her parents after being a “runt”, or a [[Main Plot/Creatures/Braincrawler\|Braincrawler]] who showed little to no promise of surviving. She managed to crawl all the way to a town, eventually taking over the mind of a small little girl.

He managed to keep himself hidden and avoid capture while the uprising of Braincrawlers was growing and growing and becoming more of an issue, and when his host body was around twenty five years old he finally shed her skin off and  true form sprout out.
While eating, a braincrawler hunter managed to injure them so greatly they had to go into a very long hibernation until they met a perfumer who properly nursed them back to health.

Mirage and this Perfumer grew incredibly close with each other, beginning to date after just one year of friendship.. however, this perfumer was unaware of Mirage’s violent tendencies.

Mirage still mostly acted human due to their upbringing within human culture, but still didn’t fully grasp what taking lives could do. When the Perfumer found out they were the cause of the thirty deaths within their village, he tried to run away.

Mirage hit him atop the head with a rock, keeping him trapped in a cage below a library until he took his own life with a small dagger he had fashioned himself, refusing to die of starvation.
by the way, Hyacinths old village was in fact old Pruina! ^^

Mirage believed that Hyacinth dying was selfish- leaving them alone and hurting. They vowed to never let anyone close leave them again, no matter the cost.
